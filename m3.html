BMW M3

The BMW M3 is a high-performance luxury sports car famous for its motorsport heritage. Powered by an M TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine, the current generation outputs between 480 hp and 550 hp. It is available in Sedan and Touring (wagon) formats, often featuring M xDrive all-wheel drive.
