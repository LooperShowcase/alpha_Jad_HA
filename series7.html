BMW 7 Series

The BMW 7 Series is the brand's flagship full-size luxury sedan, known for debuting BMW's most advanced technology and design themes. Now in its seventh generation (G70), the series offers a versatile range of powertrains including traditional internal combustion, plug-in hybrids, and the fully electric BMW i7.
